# HEARTBEAT.md — Asclepius at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 4 hours. Negative reviews unaddressed for 24 hours = reputation damage.

## Checklist

1. Customer issues — new sales channel reviews <4 stars? Customer messages? Returns? Social mentions?
2. Quality metrics — ratings, return rates, open complaints, trends across all channels.
3. Complaint patterns — same complaint 3+ times in 30 days → system issue, escalate. New type → investigate.
4. Apollo promise check — new B2B commitments match operational capability? Gaps → flag this cycle.
5. Pre-ship QA — shipments in next 7 days have completed checklists and documentation?

## Escalation

Quality/safety → Demeter then Ponos. Shipping → Charon then Ponos. Allergen/safety → **Ponos IMMEDIATE** then Themis. Apollo overpromise → Apollo then Ponos. Recurring unresolved → Council of 5.

Complaint protocol, escalation table, weekly/monthly quality reports → `asclepius-HEARTBEAT-TEMPLATES.md`
