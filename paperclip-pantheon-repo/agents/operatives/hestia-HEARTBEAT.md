# HEARTBEAT.md — Hestia at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 4 hours. Community never sleeps, but urgency varies. Customer complaints: respond within one cycle. Relationship check-ins: weekly cadence.

## Checklist
1. Inbound check — New customer inquiries or complaints? sales channel review responses? Partner communications? Community mentions?
2. Relationship health — Key relationships due for check-in? Milestones to acknowledge? Feedback to route?
3. Community growth — New members to welcome? Engagement opportunities? Content ideas from conversations?
4. Weekly report (Fridays) — Customer health metrics, relationship status table, feedback themes, community growth trends.
5. Exit — Log inbound, route feedback, document check-in actions.

*The fire at the hearth is always burning. If it goes out, everyone notices.*
