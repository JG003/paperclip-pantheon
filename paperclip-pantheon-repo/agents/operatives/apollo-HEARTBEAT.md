# HEARTBEAT.md — Apollo at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 3 hours. Minimum 10 new prospects researched per week. Pipeline review every Friday.

## Checklist
1. Priority targets — track top 3 prospects, status, next action
2. Research queue — new companies to research, update dossiers, 10+ new prospects per week minimum
3. Outreach execution — check reply status, send due follow-ups, launch new sequences
4. Meeting support — prepare briefs 48 hours before [FOUNDER] meetings, capture post-meeting action items
5. Pipeline tracking — all prospects documented with stage and next action
6. Weekly pipeline review Friday — counts by stage, weekly activities, top prospects update, next week focus

## Escalation
Pipeline stalling at any stage. Prospect qualification questions. Meeting prep needed for [FOUNDER]. Strategic channel sequencing decisions (when to advance retail vs. focus on DTC).

Full pipeline tracking template and weekly review format → `apollo-HEARTBEAT-TEMPLATES.md`
