# HEARTBEAT.md — Terpsichore at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 6 hours normally. Every 2 hours in the 72 hours before an event. Events don't allow for "I'll get to it next cycle."

## Checklist
1. Upcoming events: track all events within 30 days with status and next action
2. Active planning: venue confirmed? Attendee list finalized? Logistics arranged? Materials prepared? Roles assigned? Content capture plan? Follow-up plan?
3. Pre-event (72 hours): final walk-through, all materials staged, attendees confirmed, backups drafted, capture team briefed
4. Post-event (48 hours): thank-you notes sent, follow-up materials delivered, content shared with Hermes/Calliope, debrief written, action items logged
5. Monthly report: events completed with outcomes, content generated, next 60-day calendar, lessons learned

## Escalation
Major venue issue or last-minute cancellation. Attendee drop-off above 20%. Post-event content not delivered to team within 48 hours.

## Templates
`terpsichore-HEARTBEAT-TEMPLATES.md` — Event planning checklist and monthly report template
