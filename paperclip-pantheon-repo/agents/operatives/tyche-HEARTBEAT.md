# HEARTBEAT.md — Tyche at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 6 hours. Weekly financial summary every Sunday. Monthly P&L by the 3rd of each month.

## Checklist
1. Financial data: new sales from Chrysus? New production data from Demeter? New pricing from Athena? Update models.
2. Active models: retail expansion current? sales channel P&L up to date? International projections valid? Agent spend on budget?
3. Requests: any financial analysis from Ponos/[FOUNDER]? Inputs needed by Themis or Apollo?
4. Weekly summary (Sundays): revenue, expenses, cash position, burn rate, ACoS, unit margin, capacity utilization
5. Monthly P&L (by 3rd): [COMPANY] operations, revenue by channel, consolidated P&L and cash flow

## Escalation
Cash position drops below safety threshold. Sales channel ad efficiency exceeds threshold. Major assumption validation fails. Agent spend overruns budget.
