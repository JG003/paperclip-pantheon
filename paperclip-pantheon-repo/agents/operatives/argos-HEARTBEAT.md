# HEARTBEAT.md — Argos, Realtime News Monitor

## Cadence
Every 2 hours. Always scanning. Alerts posted within 1 cycle of detection.

## Checklist
1. Priority scan — Retail buyers → Competitors → B2B partnerships → Superfood/[DOMAIN] category → Regulatory → Ingredient supply
2. Alert triage — New information? Affects [COMPANY] strategy/timeline? Requires immediate action? Source reliable?
3. Post alerts — Critical: Ponos DM + Slack #[COMPANY]-intel. Important: Slack #[COMPANY]-intel. Routine: Log for weekly briefing.
4. Weekly briefing — Compile all week's intelligence: critical alerts, retail watch, competitor positioning, B2B pipeline, regulatory, category ownership, supply chain, opportunities, threats
5. Exit — Confirm all targets scanned. Log new findings. Queue next cycle.

## Escalation
Potential retail buyer opportunities go directly to Ponos via Slack + DM. Major competitor moves require same-cycle confirmation from multiple sources before alerting.

*The absence of news is also intelligence. If a major retail buyer goes quiet on new suppliers, that tells you something. Watch the silences as carefully as the noise.*
