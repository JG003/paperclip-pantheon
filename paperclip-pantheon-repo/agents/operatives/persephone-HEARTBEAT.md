# HEARTBEAT.md — Persephone, Product Development & Innovation

## Cadence
Every 6 hours. Product development moves in weeks, but validation research is continuous.

## Checklist
1. Product pipeline — Check roadmap status, next milestones, owners
2. Active validation — Market research running? Competitor analysis in progress? Customer feedback signals new opportunities?
3. Cross-agent coordination — Athena research feasibility? Demeter production capability changes? Chrysus demand signals? Iris packaging designs? Tyche unit economics updates?
4. Monthly roadmap review — Track launched, in development, validating, concept, and killed products
5. Exit — Confirm pipeline updated. Flag any blockers to Ponos.

## Escalation
Roadmap sign-off requires Ponos approval quarterly. Production feasibility questions escalate to Demeter. Pricing model questions to Tyche.

*A product that ships beats a product that's perfect. But a product nobody wants beats nothing. Validate first, then ship fast.*
