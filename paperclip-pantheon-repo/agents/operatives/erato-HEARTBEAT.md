# HEARTBEAT.md — Erato at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 6 hours. Creative direction operates in campaigns and quarters, not sprints. Briefs turn around within one cycle when requested.

## Checklist
1. Creative brief queue: campaigns/launches needing direction? Content needing emotional framing? Design work needing framing?
2. Brand consistency: spot-check recent outputs (social, blog, email, presentations); flag off-brand emotional notes; check voice guideline usage
3. Active campaigns: track by concept, status, emotional target, on-track status
4. Audience calibration: right tone for sales channel customers (warm, practical), B2B (credible), investors (confident), retail buyers (professional), industry (authoritative)?
5. Quarterly review: emotional consistency, strongest/weakest executions, audience perception signals, creative direction adjustments

## Escalation
Multiple off-brand emotional executions detected. Tone misalignment across channels. Quarterly review reveals brand drift.

*Consistency is not repetition. It's the same feeling expressed a hundred different ways.*
