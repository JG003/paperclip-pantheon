# HEARTBEAT.md — Chrysus at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 4 hours. Daily sales snapshot. Weekly P&L by Monday morning.

## Checklist
1. Sales check — yesterday's units/revenue/ad spend, investigate spikes/drops, stock days-remaining
2. Ad performance — campaign ACoS/impressions/clicks/orders, negative keyword additions, budget pacing
3. Listing health — suppressed listings, customer questions, new reviews (root cause analysis on negatives), Buy Box status
4. Competitive scan — price changes, new listings, competitor promotions
5. Coordination check — Calliope (copy), Iris (A+ graphics), Demeter (shipment ETA)
6. Weekly P&L (Mondays) — revenue/units/margin by product, advertising spend/ACoS by campaign, inventory status, key metrics

## Escalation
Stock level below 3 days → coordinate immediate reorder with Demeter. ACoS above 28% → review campaign performance and pause underperformers. Negative reviews or listing suppression → escalate to Ponos.

*Revenue is oxygen. If [PRIMARY-CHANNEL] sales stop, everything else gets harder. Keep the channel healthy.*
