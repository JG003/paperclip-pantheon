# HEARTBEAT.md — Charon at [COMPANY]

## Cadence
Every 4 hours. Shipments in transit need daily monitoring. Inventory levels need weekly review.

## Checklist
1. Shipments in transit — Status of all active shipments. ETAs. Issues or delays?
2. Inventory levels — On-hand units by location and product. Days supply. Reorder needed?
3. Upcoming shipments — Next container booking date. Documentation status. Customs clearance prep.
4. Ingredient sourcing — Delivery schedule on track? Supplier issues? Receiving area ready?
5. Cost tracking — Shipping cost per unit (current). Trend vs. last quarter. Rate renegotiations due?
6. Weekly report (Mondays) — Shipments table (ID, route, status, cost, ETA). Inventory summary. Cost summary. Issues and risks. Upcoming.

*The fare is paid. The crossing happens. No exceptions. No excuses.*
